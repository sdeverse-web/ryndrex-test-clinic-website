About Elegance Cosmetic Surgery

Dedicated to Excellence, Artistry, and Patient Satisfaction Since 2008

Our Story

Elegance Cosmetic Surgery was founded on the principle that every patient deserves exceptional care combined with artistic excellence. Over the past 18 years, we have established ourselves as a leading provider of cosmetic and reconstructive surgery, serving thousands of satisfied patients who have entrusted us with their aesthetic goals.

Our clinic was built with a vision: to create a welcoming environment where patients feel heard, respected, and confident in their decision to pursue cosmetic enhancement. We believe that beauty is not about conforming to standards, but about bringing out the best version of yourself.

From our state-of-the-art surgical facility to our compassionate support staff, every aspect of Elegance Cosmetic Surgery is designed with you in mind. We combine cutting-edge surgical techniques with a deep commitment to natural-looking results and patient satisfaction.

Our Philosophy

Natural Results

We believe in enhancing your natural beauty, not creating an artificial appearance. Our surgeons focus on subtle refinements that bring out your best features while maintaining your unique identity.

Patient-Centered Care

Your goals, concerns, and comfort are at the center of everything we do. We take time to listen, educate, and collaborate with you to ensure your expectations are met or exceeded.

Artistic Excellence

Cosmetic surgery is both a science and an art. Our surgeons combine technical expertise with an artistic eye to create beautiful, harmonious results that enhance your overall appearance.

Safety First

We maintain the highest standards of safety and hygiene. Our facility is fully accredited, and our team follows rigorous protocols to ensure your well-being at every stage of your journey.

Continuous Learning

Our surgeons stay at the forefront of cosmetic surgery innovation through ongoing education and training. We invest in the latest techniques and technologies to provide you with the best possible outcomes.

Ethical Practice

We operate with integrity and transparency. We provide honest assessments, realistic expectations, and will never pressure you into procedures you don't want or need.

Meet Our Surgeons

Dr. Victoria Sterling, MD - Chief Surgeon & Founder

Dr. Victoria Sterling Photo

Specialties: Facial Rejuvenation, Rhinoplasty, Facelift, Brow Lift

Dr. Victoria Sterling founded Elegance Cosmetic Surgery with a mission to provide world-class aesthetic care. With 18 years of experience in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery, she has earned a reputation as a leading expert in facial aesthetics.

Dr. Sterling completed her medical degree at prestigious institutions and her plastic surgery residency at a top-tier teaching hospital. She has trained surgeons across North America and regularly speaks at international conferences on advanced surgical techniques.

Board Certifications:

  • American Board of Plastic Surgery
  • American Society of Plastic Surgeons
  • International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery

Recognition: Named "Top Cosmetic Surgeon" by Metropolitan Medical Magazine for five consecutive years.

Dr. James Mitchell, MD - Breast Surgery Specialist

Dr. James Mitchell Photo

Specialties: Breast Augmentation, Breast Lift, Breast Reconstruction, Revision Surgery

Dr. James Mitchell is a highly skilled breast surgery specialist with 14 years of experience. His artistic approach and meticulous attention to detail have made him a sought-after surgeon for breast enhancement procedures.

Dr. Mitchell is known for his ability to create natural-looking results that complement each patient's unique body proportions and aesthetic goals. He stays current with the latest breast surgery techniques and implant technologies.

Board Certifications:

  • American Board of Plastic Surgery
  • American Society of Plastic Surgeons
  • Breast Surgery Specialty Board

Recognition: Published researcher with multiple peer-reviewed articles on breast surgery outcomes and patient satisfaction.

Dr. Elena Rodriguez, MD - Body Contouring Expert

Dr. Elena Rodriguez Photo

Specialties: Liposuction, Tummy Tuck, Body Lift, Mommy Makeover, Liposuction Revision

Dr. Elena Rodriguez specializes in body contouring procedures and has helped thousands of patients achieve their aesthetic goals. With 12 years of experience, she is known for her innovative approach to body sculpting and her commitment to natural-looking results.

Dr. Rodriguez combines advanced liposuction techniques with comprehensive body contouring to create beautiful, proportionate silhouettes. She is particularly passionate about helping post-pregnancy patients restore their pre-pregnancy figures.

Board Certifications:

Recognition: Featured speaker at national plastic surgery conferences on advanced body contouring techniques.

Facility Accreditation

Our surgical facility is fully accredited by the American Association for Accreditation of Ambulatory Surgery Facilities (AAAASF), ensuring compliance with the highest standards of safety and quality.

Board Certification

Professional Memberships

Our team maintains active memberships in leading professional organizations including the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.

Continuing Education

We are committed to ongoing professional development, with our surgeons regularly attending conferences and training programs to stay current with the latest techniques and technologies.

Our Commitment to Patient Care

From Consultation to Recovery

We believe that exceptional patient care extends far beyond the operating room. Our team is dedicated to supporting you at every stage of your cosmetic surgery journey:

  • Thorough Consultation: We take time to understand your goals and answer all your questions.
  • Personalized Planning: Each treatment plan is customized to your unique anatomy and aesthetic goals.
  • Expert Surgical Care: Our surgeons use advanced techniques to achieve beautiful, natural results.
  • Comprehensive Aftercare: We provide detailed post-operative instructions and ongoing support during your recovery.
  • Long-Term Follow-Up: We maintain contact with our patients to ensure lasting satisfaction and address any concerns.

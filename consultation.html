The Consultation Process

Step 1: Initial Assessment
We'll review your medical history, current medications, and any previous surgeries. This helps us ensure you're a good candidate for your desired procedure.
Step 2: Goal Discussion
Our surgeon will listen carefully to your aesthetic goals and concerns. We'll discuss what you like and don't like about your appearance and what you hope to achieve.
Step 3: Physical Examination
A thorough physical examination will be performed to assess your anatomy and determine the best surgical approach for your goals.
Step 4: Imaging and Visualization
We may use advanced 3D imaging technology to show you potential results and help you visualize the changes that could be made.
Step 5: Treatment Plan
Based on your goals and anatomy, we'll create a personalized treatment plan including procedure details, recovery timeline, and cost estimates.
Step 6: Q&A and Next Steps
You'll have the opportunity to ask any questions. If you decide to proceed, we'll discuss scheduling and pre-operative preparation.

Consultation FAQs

Is there a consultation fee?
We offer free initial consultations for most procedures. This gives you the opportunity to meet our surgeons and discuss your goals without any financial commitment.
How long does a consultation take?
Most consultations take 45 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of your goals and the number of procedures you're considering.
What should I bring to my consultation?
Bring a valid ID and any insurance information if applicable. You may also want to bring photos of results you like or areas you want to discuss.
Can I bring someone with me?
Yes, you're welcome to bring a trusted friend or family member to your consultation for support and to help you remember important information.
What if I'm not ready to schedule surgery?
That's perfectly fine. A consultation is meant to provide information and help you make an informed decision. There's no pressure to proceed with surgery.
How do I schedule a consultation?
Simply fill out the consultation request form above, call us at (555) 123-4567, or email us at info@elegancecosmetic.com. We'll contact you within 24 business hours to confirm your appointment.

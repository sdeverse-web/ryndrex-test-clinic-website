What is a Mommy Makeover?

A mommy makeover is a customized combination of surgical procedures designed to restore your body after pregnancy and childbirth. Pregnancy and nursing can dramatically change a woman's body, affecting the breasts, abdomen, and overall body contours. A mommy makeover typically combines breast surgery with body contouring to help you reclaim your pre-pregnancy figure.

Our surgeons understand the unique challenges mothers face and are dedicated to helping you feel confident and beautiful again.

Typical Mommy Makeover Procedures

Breast Augmentation or Lift

Restore breast volume lost during pregnancy and nursing, or lift sagging breasts for a more youthful appearance.

Tummy Tuck

Remove excess abdominal skin and tighten separated muscles to restore a flatter, more defined midsection.

Liposuction

Remove stubborn fat deposits that didn't respond to diet and exercise, enhancing overall body contours.

Customization

Every mommy makeover is customized to your specific concerns and goals. We can add or modify procedures based on your needs.

Ideal Candidates for Mommy Makeover

Mommy makeover is suitable for mothers who:

  • Have completed their family or are not planning future pregnancies
  • Are unhappy with post-pregnancy body changes
  • Have excess abdominal skin or separated muscles
  • Have lost breast volume or have sagging breasts
  • Have stubborn fat deposits resistant to diet and exercise
  • Are in good overall health
  • Have realistic expectations about surgical outcomes
  • Are committed to maintaining their results through healthy lifestyle

During your consultation, our surgeons will assess your candidacy and discuss which procedures are right for you.

Recovery and Aftercare

First Week

You'll experience soreness, swelling, and bruising at multiple surgical sites. Pain is managed with prescribed medication. You'll wear compression garments for support.

Weeks 2-4

Most patients can return to light activities and desk work. Avoid strenuous exercise and heavy lifting. Swelling continues to decrease.

Weeks 4-6

You can gradually resume normal activities, including light exercise. Most of the discomfort has resolved.

Months 2-3

You can return to all activities, including strenuous exercise. Final results become apparent as swelling fully resolves.

Post-Operative Instructions

  • Wear compression garments as directed for proper support
  • Keep your head elevated when resting to minimize swelling
  • Take prescribed medications as directed
  • Avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for 4-6 weeks
  • Don't bend or twist at the waist for the first few weeks
  • Avoid swimming and hot tubs until incisions are fully healed
  • Arrange childcare support during your recovery
  • Attend all follow-up appointments

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I breastfeed after mommy makeover?
Breast augmentation typically does not affect breastfeeding. However, breast lift may impact milk production in some cases. Discuss this with your surgeon during your consultation.
How long is the surgery?
Mommy makeover typically takes 3 to 5 hours, depending on which procedures are included. Most patients stay overnight for observation.
When can I return to normal activities?
Most patients can return to light activities within 2-3 weeks and resume normal activities within 4-6 weeks. However, strenuous exercise should be avoided for at least 6 weeks.
Will mommy makeover scars be visible?
Incisions are strategically placed to minimize visibility. Most scars fade significantly over time and are hidden by clothing or swimwear.
Can I have future pregnancies after mommy makeover?
While you can become pregnant after mommy makeover, pregnancy will stretch the skin and muscles again, potentially affecting your results. Most surgeons recommend completing your family before this procedure.

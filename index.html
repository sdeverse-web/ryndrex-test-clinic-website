Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recovery time for cosmetic surgery?
Recovery times vary depending on the procedure. Most patients can return to light activities within 1-2 weeks, with full recovery typically occurring within 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed post-operative instructions for each procedure.
Is cosmetic surgery safe?
Yes, cosmetic surgery is safe when performed by board-certified surgeons in accredited facilities. Our team follows strict safety protocols and uses state-of-the-art technology to minimize risks.
How much does cosmetic surgery cost?
Costs vary based on the procedure and individual factors. During your consultation, we provide a detailed cost breakdown and discuss financing options to make procedures accessible.
Can I combine multiple procedures?
Yes, many patients choose to combine procedures for comprehensive results. Our surgeons will assess your goals and recommend the best approach during your consultation.

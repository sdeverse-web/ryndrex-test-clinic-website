Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?
You can schedule a consultation by calling us at (555) 123-4567, emailing info@elegancecosmetic.com, or filling out the consultation request form on our website. We'll contact you within 24 business hours to confirm your appointment.
What should I expect during my first visit?
During your first visit, you'll meet with our staff, complete a brief health questionnaire, and then have a private consultation with one of our surgeons. The entire process typically takes 45 minutes to an hour.
Do you accept insurance?
Most cosmetic procedures are not covered by insurance as they are elective. However, we offer flexible financing options to make procedures more accessible. Contact us for more information.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. We also offer financing options through partner companies to help make your procedure more affordable.
Can I tour the facility before scheduling surgery?
Yes, we're happy to give you a tour of our facility during your consultation. This helps you become familiar with our state-of-the-art surgical center.
How can I stay updated with news and specials?
You can stay updated by following us on social media or signing up for our newsletter. We regularly share patient education content, special offers, and clinic updates.

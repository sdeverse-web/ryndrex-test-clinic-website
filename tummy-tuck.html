What is a Tummy Tuck?

A tummy tuck, or abdominoplasty, is a surgical procedure that removes excess skin and fat from the abdomen while tightening the underlying abdominal muscles. This procedure is ideal for individuals who have loose, sagging skin around the midsection due to pregnancy, significant weight loss, or natural aging.

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in creating beautifully sculpted abdomens that are proportionate to your body and enhance your overall silhouette.

Procedure Overview

Surgical Technique

A tummy tuck involves an incision across the lower abdomen, typically hidden by swimwear or underwear. Excess skin is removed, and abdominal muscles are tightened for improved contour.

Duration

The procedure typically takes 2 to 3 hours, depending on the extent of the surgery. Most patients stay overnight for observation and go home the next day.

Anesthesia

Tummy tuck is performed under general anesthesia, ensuring your comfort and safety throughout the procedure.

Customization

From mini tummy tucks to comprehensive procedures, we offer options tailored to your specific needs and the amount of excess skin.

Ideal Candidates for Tummy Tuck

Tummy tuck is suitable for individuals who:

  • Have excess abdominal skin and fat
  • Have loose or sagging skin after pregnancy or weight loss
  • Have separated or weakened abdominal muscles
  • Want a flatter, more contoured midsection
  • Are in good overall health
  • Have realistic expectations about surgical outcomes
  • Are not planning future pregnancies (or understand the implications)
  • Maintain a stable weight

During your consultation, Dr. Rodriguez will assess your candidacy and discuss whether tummy tuck is the right choice for you.

Benefits of Tummy Tuck

Flatter Abdomen

Tummy tuck removes excess skin and fat, creating a noticeably flatter, more sculpted midsection.

Improved Muscle Definition

Tightening of abdominal muscles creates better definition and a more toned appearance.

Enhanced Silhouette

A flatter abdomen enhances your overall body proportions and allows you to wear clothing more confidently.

Long-Lasting Results

Tummy tuck results are permanent, provided you maintain a stable weight and healthy lifestyle.

Improved Posture

Tightened abdominal muscles can improve posture and reduce back strain.

Boosted Confidence

Many patients report a significant increase in confidence and body satisfaction after tummy tuck.

Recovery and Aftercare

First Week

You'll experience soreness, swelling, and tightness in the abdomen. Pain is managed with prescribed medication. You'll wear a compression garment for support.

Weeks 2-4

Most patients can return to light activities and desk work. Avoid strenuous exercise and heavy lifting. Swelling continues to decrease.

Weeks 4-6

You can gradually resume normal activities, including light exercise. Most of the discomfort has resolved.

Months 2-3

You can return to all activities, including strenuous exercise. Final results become apparent as swelling fully resolves.

Post-Operative Instructions

  • Wear the compression garment as directed for proper support
  • Keep your head elevated when resting to minimize swelling
  • Take prescribed medications as directed
  • Avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for 4-6 weeks
  • Don't bend or twist at the waist for the first few weeks
  • Avoid swimming and hot tubs until incisions are fully healed
  • Attend all follow-up appointments
  • Report any unusual symptoms to your surgeon immediately

Frequently Asked Questions

Will tummy tuck scars be visible?
The incision is typically placed low on the abdomen, hidden by swimwear or underwear. Scars fade significantly over time and become less noticeable.
Can I have future pregnancies after tummy tuck?
While you can become pregnant after tummy tuck, pregnancy will stretch the skin and muscles again, potentially affecting your results. It's best to complete your family before having this procedure.
How long do tummy tuck results last?
Tummy tuck results are permanent, provided you maintain a stable weight and healthy lifestyle. Significant weight gain or pregnancy can affect your results.
Can tummy tuck be combined with other procedures?
Yes, many patients combine tummy tuck with liposuction, breast augmentation, or other procedures for comprehensive body contouring.
What are the risks of tummy tuck?
Like any surgery, tummy tuck carries some risks, including infection, bleeding, and fluid accumulation. However, serious complications are rare when performed by a board-certified surgeon.

What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a surgical procedure that removes excess fat deposits from specific areas of the body. Whether you have stubborn fat that doesn't respond to diet and exercise, or you want to refine your body contours, liposuction can help you achieve your aesthetic goals.

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in advanced liposuction techniques that create smooth, natural-looking results while enhancing your overall body proportions.

Procedure Overview

Treatment Areas

Liposuction can be performed on virtually any area of the body, including the abdomen, flanks, thighs, arms, back, chin, and more. Multiple areas can be treated during a single procedure.

Advanced Techniques

We utilize tumescent liposuction and ultrasound-assisted techniques for precise fat removal with minimal trauma to surrounding tissues.

Duration

The procedure typically takes 1 to 3 hours, depending on the number of areas treated. Most patients go home the same day.

Anesthesia

Liposuction can be performed under local, twilight, or general anesthesia, depending on the extent of the procedure.

Ideal Candidates for Liposuction

Liposuction is suitable for individuals who:

  • Have localized fat deposits that don't respond to diet and exercise
  • Want to refine and sculpt specific body areas
  • Are at or near their ideal body weight
  • Have good skin elasticity
  • Are in good overall health
  • Have realistic expectations about surgical outcomes
  • Are committed to maintaining their results through healthy lifestyle
  • Have specific problem areas they want to address

During your consultation, Dr. Rodriguez will assess your candidacy and discuss whether liposuction is the right choice for you.

Benefits of Liposuction

Precise Fat Removal

Liposuction targets specific areas, removing stubborn fat deposits that are resistant to diet and exercise.

Improved Body Contours

By removing excess fat, liposuction enhances your natural body shape and creates more defined contours.

Enhanced Proportions

Liposuction can improve overall body proportions and balance, complementing your natural physique.

Boosted Confidence

Many patients report a significant increase in confidence and body satisfaction after liposuction.

Minimal Scarring

Incisions are small and strategically placed, resulting in minimal, well-hidden scarring.

Long-Lasting Results

Liposuction results are long-lasting, provided you maintain a healthy weight and lifestyle.

Recovery and Aftercare

First Week

You'll experience soreness, swelling, and bruising at the treatment sites. Pain is managed with prescribed medication. You'll wear a compression garment for support.

Weeks 2-3

Most patients can return to light activities and desk work. Swelling continues to decrease. Avoid strenuous exercise.

Weeks 4-6

You can gradually resume normal activities, including light exercise. Most of the discomfort has resolved.

Months 2-3

You can return to all activities, including strenuous exercise. Final results become apparent as swelling fully resolves.

Post-Operative Instructions

  • Wear the compression garment as directed for proper support
  • Take prescribed medications as directed
  • Avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for 3-4 weeks
  • Avoid swimming and hot tubs until incisions are fully healed
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy diet
  • Perform gentle movement to promote healing
  • Attend all follow-up appointments
  • Report any unusual symptoms to your surgeon immediately

Frequently Asked Questions

Will liposuction scars be visible?
Incisions are small and strategically placed in natural creases or inconspicuous areas. Scars fade significantly over time and are typically not noticeable.
Can the fat return after liposuction?
The fat cells removed during liposuction do not return. However, if you gain weight after the procedure, remaining fat cells in the treated areas can enlarge.
How much fat can be removed?
The amount of fat removed depends on your anatomy, health, and aesthetic goals. Dr. Rodriguez will discuss realistic expectations during your consultation.
Can liposuction be combined with other procedures?
Yes, many patients combine liposuction with tummy tuck, breast augmentation, or other procedures for comprehensive body contouring.
What are the risks of liposuction?
Like any surgery, liposuction carries some risks, including infection, bleeding, and contour irregularities. However, serious complications are rare when performed by a board-certified surgeon.

What is Rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty, commonly known as a "nose job," is a surgical procedure that reshapes and refines the nose to improve its appearance and, in some cases, its function. Whether you're looking to correct a bump, reduce the size of your nose, refine the tip, or achieve better facial balance, rhinoplasty can deliver transformative results.

Our surgeons combine artistic vision with surgical precision to create natural-looking results that enhance your overall facial harmony while maintaining your unique identity.

Procedure Overview

Surgical Approach

Rhinoplasty can be performed using either an open or closed technique. The open approach provides better visibility for complex reshaping, while the closed approach minimizes visible scarring. Dr. Sterling will recommend the best approach for your specific goals.

Duration

The procedure typically takes 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on the complexity of the reshaping required. Most patients are able to return home the same day.

Anesthesia

Rhinoplasty is performed under general anesthesia, ensuring your comfort and safety throughout the procedure. Our anesthesiologists monitor your vitals continuously.

Customization

Every rhinoplasty is customized to your unique facial structure and aesthetic goals. We use advanced imaging to help you visualize potential results before surgery.

Ideal Candidates for Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty is suitable for individuals who:

  • Are unhappy with the size or shape of their nose
  • Have a prominent bump or bridge
  • Want to refine the nasal tip
  • Desire improved facial balance and proportion
  • Have breathing difficulties due to structural issues
  • Are in good overall health
  • Have realistic expectations about surgical outcomes
  • Are at least 16 years old (ideally after facial growth is complete)

During your consultation, Dr. Sterling will assess your candidacy and discuss whether rhinoplasty is the right choice for you.

Benefits of Rhinoplasty

Enhanced Facial Harmony

A refined nose creates better facial balance and proportion, enhancing your overall appearance and natural beauty.

Improved Confidence

Many patients report a significant boost in self-confidence and self-esteem after rhinoplasty, allowing them to feel more comfortable in social and professional settings.

Our surgeons focus on creating results that look natural and complement your facial features, not artificial or overdone.

Personalized Approach

Recovery and Aftercare

First Week

You'll experience some swelling and bruising around the nose and eyes. A splint will protect your nose, and pain is typically managed with prescribed medication. Most patients can return to light activities after 3-5 days.

Weeks 2-4

Swelling continues to decrease, though some residual puffiness may remain. You can gradually return to normal activities, though strenuous exercise should be avoided for at least 3-4 weeks.

Months 2-6

Most of the swelling subsides, and you'll begin to see your final results taking shape. The nose continues to refine as internal healing progresses.

Full Results

Complete healing and final results typically appear after 12 months, though most patients are very happy with their appearance much sooner.

Post-Operative Instructions

  • Keep your head elevated to minimize swelling
  • Apply ice packs as directed to reduce bruising
  • Avoid strenuous activities and heavy lifting for 3-4 weeks
  • Don't blow your nose forcefully for at least one week
  • Avoid contact sports and activities that could injure your nose
  • Attend all follow-up appointments
  • Take prescribed medications as directed

Frequently Asked Questions

Will rhinoplasty leave visible scars?
With the closed rhinoplasty technique, all incisions are made inside the nose, leaving no visible scars. The open technique involves a small incision on the columella (the tissue between the nostrils), which typically heals very well and is barely noticeable.
Can rhinoplasty correct breathing problems?
Yes, rhinoplasty can address structural issues that impede breathing, such as a deviated septum or enlarged turbinates. This is often combined with aesthetic improvements for comprehensive results.
How long do rhinoplasty results last?
Rhinoplasty results are permanent. The structural changes made to your nose are lasting, though minor changes may occur as you age.
Can I wear glasses after rhinoplasty?
You should avoid wearing glasses for at least 4 weeks after surgery to prevent pressure on the healing nose. Consider using contact lenses during this period, or use a special glasses holder that doesn't rest on your nose.
What are the risks of rhinoplasty?
Like any surgery, rhinoplasty carries some risks, including infection, bleeding, and anesthesia complications. However, serious complications are rare when the procedure is performed by a board-certified surgeon in an accredited facility.
